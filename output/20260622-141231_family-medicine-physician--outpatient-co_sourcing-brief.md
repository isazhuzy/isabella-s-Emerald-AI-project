## 2. Target search criteria
- **Titles:** Family Medicine Physician, Family Practice Physician, Family Physician, Primary Care Physician, Outpatient Family Medicine Physician
- **Location:** Dallas–Fort Worth, TX (Westside clinic); relocation candidates considered
- **Seniority:** Attending physician; new graduates through experienced welcome
- **Skills:** Outpatient/ambulatory care, Continuity primary care, Epic EHR, Team-based care, Value-based care, Chronic disease management, Bilingual Spanish
- **Must-have:** MD or DO; Board Certified or Board Eligible in Family Medicine; Active TX license or ability to obtain; DEA license or eligibility; Willingness to practice 100% outpatient (no OB)
- **Nice-to-have:** Bilingual Spanish; Value-based care experience; Epic experience; Comfort in team-based care with nurse care managers

## 3. Runbook (the one manual step is the search itself)
1. Open this job in Loxo and run each channel — **Loxo Source**, **LinkedIn OW**,
   **LinkedIn Non-OW (more passive)**, **Indeed Database** — using the matching
   Boolean above (or Loxo AI search with the criteria).
2. Review results; select the best **75–150** candidates across channels.
3. **Bulk-move them to the `Long List` stage.** (No enrichment
   here — keeps it free for the long list.)
4. Pick the top **20** and move them to the `Short List` stage.
   → Stage Automation fires: **find contact details (enrich)** + **add to the
   `Confidential Outreach` campaign** + **AI highlights**.
5. Review enriched contacts + AI highlights, then let the campaign send.

_Enrichment is capped to the top 20 by only moving them into
`Short List` — this is the cost-control gate._
