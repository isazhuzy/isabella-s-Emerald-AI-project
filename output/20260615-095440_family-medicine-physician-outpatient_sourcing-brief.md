# Sourcing Brief — Family Medicine Physician (Outpatient)

> Confidential search. Do not expose the client name in any posting or outreach.

## 1. Paste into Loxo Source
```
family medicine physician board certified outpatient
```
Other Boolean variants (for LinkedIn / Google X-ray / re-use):
- **generic:** `("family medicine" OR "family practice") AND (physician OR MD OR DO) AND ("board certified" OR "board eligible") NOT (resident OR locum)`
- **linkedin:** `("Family Medicine" OR "Family Practice") AND (Physician OR MD OR DO)`
- **google_xray:** `site:linkedin.com/in ("family medicine") (physician OR MD OR DO)`

## 2. Target search criteria
- **Titles:** Family Medicine Physician, Primary Care Physician
- **Location:** (from transcript)
- **Seniority:** Attending
- **Skills:** outpatient care, EHR documentation, preventive medicine
- **Must-have:** BC/BE Family Medicine; active/eligible license
- **Nice-to-have:** bilingual; value-based care experience

## 3. Runbook (the one manual step is the search itself)
1. Open this job in Loxo → **Loxo Source**.
2. Paste the Boolean above (or use AI search with the criteria) and run it.
3. Review results; select the best **75–150** candidates.
4. **Bulk-move them to the `Sourcing` stage.** (No enrichment
   here — keeps it free for the long list.)
5. Pick the top **20** and move them to the `Outreach` stage.
   → Stage Automation fires: **find contact details (enrich)** + **add to the
   `Confidential Outreach` campaign** + **AI highlights**.
6. Review enriched contacts + AI highlights, then let the campaign send.

_Enrichment is capped to the top 20 by only moving them into
`Outreach` — this is the cost-control gate._
